Ham Towers

Portfolio
September 07, 2019 12:43 pm

Ham Towers is a 7-storey building that was developed in the year 2011 in downtown Kampala. The property developed on freehold land in rentable space with current occupancy rates of 95%. The current valuation of the property is over USD 73,000,000.

Ham Towers is a world class shopping, accommodation and leisure centre with offices, Shops, Supermarket and fully furnished apartments in the Ham Suites with a five Star Food Court and gym.

Located within a thirty minute drive from the city center on Makerere Hill Ham Towers offers breathtaking views of the city and the outskirts at a ordable prices depending on your needs.
