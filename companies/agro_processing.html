Ham Agro Processing Industries

Portfolio
September 07, 2019 12:43 pm

The Ugandan economy is highly dependent on agriculture with sector employing a large of low income earners. However the majority of those involved in the sector are at a subsistence level which has low returns with low contributions to the national GDP.

Ham Agro-Processing Industries will comprise of ten Integrated Agro-processing Industrial Parks (IAIPs) within the crop and livestock production zone locations in Uganda to facilitate value addition, provide market for locally grown raw materials and encourage commercialization of the sector while increasing exports

This will be in line with crop and livestock resource endowments or competitive comparative advantages speci c to each agro-ecological zone that comprise of: North-eastern Dry lands; North-eastern Savannah Grasslands; North-western Savannah Grasslands; Parra Savannahs; Kyoga Plains; Lake Victoria Crescent; Western Savannah Grasslands; Pastoral Rangelands; South-western Farmlands; and the Highland Ranges.

We shall also put up ancillary units like packaging, printing, transportation agencies, carrying and forwarding agencies among others to support the common activities of the food processing units.

The Integrated Agro-Processing Industrial Park (IAIP) will prioritize businesses dealing with high value-added fresh fruits and vegetables, food processing industry, which have comparative advantages in quality.

The East African Community (EAC) and Common Market of Eastern and Southern Africa (COMESA) regional markets as well as the European Union (EU) countriesand the Gulf States would be the most promising export markets for processed foods and fresh fruits and vegetables produced by the Ham Agro-Processing Industries IAIPs.
